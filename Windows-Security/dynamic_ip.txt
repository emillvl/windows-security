Saatte IP yenileme

schtasks /create /sc hourly /tn "IP_Renew" /tr "ipconfig /release & ipconfig /renew"

Bilgisayar açıldığında IP yenileme

schtasks /create /sc ONSTART /tn "IP_Renew" /tr "ipconfig /release & ipconfig /renew" /f

Her x dakikada bir IP yenileme(x'i istediginiz sayi ile degisin)

schtasks /create /sc MINUTE /mo x /tn "IP_Renew" /tr "ipconfig /release & ipconfig /renew" /f

Her gün saat xx:yy’te IP yenileme(03:00; 05:00 vb.)

schtasks /create /sc DAILY /st xx:yy /tn "IP_Renew" /tr "ipconfig /release & ipconfig /renew" /f


IN ENGLISH:

Hourly IP Renewal

schtasks /create /sc hourly /tn "IP_Renew" /tr "ipconfig /release & ipconfig /renew"


IP Renewal on Computer Startup

schtasks /create /sc ONSTART /tn "IP_Renew" /tr "ipconfig /release & ipconfig /renew" /f


IP Renewal Every X Minutes (replace X with your desired number)

schtasks /create /sc MINUTE /mo x /tn "IP_Renew" /tr "ipconfig /release & ipconfig /renew" /f


IP Renewal Every Day at Specific Time (e.g., 03:00; 05:00, etc.)

schtasks /create /sc DAILY /st xx:yy /tn "IP_Renew" /tr "ipconfig /release & ipconfig /renew" /f
